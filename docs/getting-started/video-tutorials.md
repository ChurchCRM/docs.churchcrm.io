## Recommended Recordings

### 🔴 High Priority

These topics involve multi-step workflows that are difficult to follow from text alone. New users consistently struggle here first.

#### 1. Getting Started: Dashboard Orientation (5–8 min)
**Covers:** Logging in for the first time, navigating the top menu, understanding the dashboard widgets, using the global search bar (press `?`), and finding the Admin menu.  
**Why:** Most users land in the app without context. A quick orientation video reduces the number of support questions about where things are.  
**Related docs:** [Welcome](/), [First Run Configuration](/getting-started/first-run)

---

#### 2. Adding Families and People (8–12 min)
**Covers:** Creating a new family record, adding family members in the same form, assigning Classifications, adding a standalone person, and editing an existing record.  
**Why:** This is the first real task every new user performs. Showing the family-first data model visually helps users understand how people relate to families.  
**Related docs:** [Families](/user-guide/families), [Persons](/user-guide/persons), [Classifications](/user-guide/classifications)

---

#### 3. Recording Attendance at an Event (8–10 min)
**Covers:** Creating an Event Type, adding a Church Event, adding people to the Cart, using "Empty Cart to Event" to record attendance, and viewing the attendance report afterward.  
**Why:** The Cart-based workflow is unique to ChurchCRM and confusing without visual context.  
**Related docs:** [Events](/user-guide/events), [Cart](/user-guide/cart)

---

#### 4. Financial Setup: Funds, Donations, and Deposits (10–15 min)
**Covers:** Creating donation funds, recording a cash/check donation on a family record, creating a deposit slip, generating a giving statement (tax letter), and running a pledge summary report.  
**Why:** Financial workflows have the most steps and the highest stakes. Errors here affect year-end tax statements.  
**Related docs:** [Finances](/user-guide/finances)

---

#### 5. Kiosk Device Setup and Check-In (10–15 min)
**Covers:** Enabling kiosk mode, registering a kiosk device, self-service family check-in flow, the Sunday School view for teachers, and parent alert/checkout.  
**Why:** Kiosk setup involves server-side configuration, a separate device workflow, and QR codes — all of which are much clearer on video.  
**Related docs:** [Kiosk Devices](/user-guide/kiosk-devices)

---

### 🟡 Medium Priority

These topics are important but the written docs are adequate for most users. Video would accelerate adoption for staff who are less technical.

#### 6. Group Management and Bulk Operations (8–10 min)
**Covers:** Creating a group with roles, searching for people, adding them to the Cart, using "Empty Cart to Group", and emailing a group.  
**Related docs:** [Groups](/user-guide/groups), [Cart](/user-guide/cart), [Email](/user-guide/email)

---

#### 7. Running Reports and Custom Queries (8–10 min)
**Covers:** Navigating the Reports Menu, generating a Family Directory, running an Attendance Report, and writing a simple custom SQL query.  
**Related docs:** [Reports & Queries](/user-guide/reports-and-queries)

---

#### 8. Email and Mailchimp Integration (6–8 min)
**Covers:** Configuring SMTP, sending a one-time group email via Cart, and syncing the congregation list to Mailchimp for a newsletter.  
**Related docs:** [Email](/user-guide/email), [Email Setup](/administration/email-setup)

---

#### 9. Backup, Restore, and Upgrading (6–8 min)
**Covers:** Creating a manual database backup, downloading it, restoring from a backup, and running the upgrade procedure.  
**Why:** These are infrequent but high-stakes operations. Users are often nervous and benefit from seeing the expected output.  
**Related docs:** [Backup & Restore](/administration/backup-restore), [Upgrade Guide](/administration/upgrade)

---

### 🟢 Lower Priority

Useful for power users and administrators, but not essential for day-one onboarding.

#### 10. Custom Fields and Properties (6–8 min)
**Covers:** Adding a custom field to persons, configuring field types (text, date, dropdown), and assigning a Property label to a group of people via the Cart.  
**Related docs:** [Custom Fields](/user-guide/custom-fields), [Properties](/user-guide/properties)

---

#### 11. Data Import from CSV (6–8 min)
**Covers:** Preparing the import CSV (required columns, family grouping), running the import wizard, and reviewing the results.  
**Related docs:** [Data Import](/user-guide/data-import)

---

#### 12. User Management and Permissions (5–6 min)
**Covers:** Creating a staff user account, assigning a role, demonstrating what each permission level can and cannot do.  
**Related docs:** [Users](/administration/users), [Security](/administration/security)

---

## Recording Tips

- Aim for **1080p screen capture** with a visible cursor.
- Use a **test/demo database** so no real member data appears on screen.
- Keep individual videos **under 15 minutes** — break longer topics into parts.
- Link each video back to the relevant documentation page and vice versa.
